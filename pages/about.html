About Us

Overview

Our business model is to identify and in-license novel therapeutic platforms / product assets with demonstrated human safety & efficacy data with durable clinical benefit (ideally, commercially marketed in select countries outside US / Europe under country-specific-FDA approval) and undertake clinical / regulatory development for commercial markets in US & Europe.

Our Core Values

MKC Biotherapeutics is focused on development of accessible, cost-effective, safe & efficacious cell-drug products that halt progression, repair damage and enable regeneration of biologically active cells and tissues; via immune-modulation of disease environment in multiple indications; thereby providing significant improvement in quality of life and enabling an active, productive and healthier life.

Our core values are exemplified by our DNA that permits us to focus on patient-centricity in how we Harness Biology to Heal Lives
