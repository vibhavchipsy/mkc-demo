Summary of Stempeucel Product Clinical Data
Stempeucel® Product has shown safety & efficacy in Knee OA & Critical Limb Ischemia (BD & PAD) trials
Significant improvement in overall WOMAC composite index (p < 0.0001) with 71.43% reduction
Significant improvement in all WOMAC subscores for pain (p < 0.0001) with 73.12% reduction, physical function (p < 0.0001) with 71.46% reduction and stiffness (p < 0.0001) with 77.25% reduction
Significant improvement in VAS score (p < 0.0001) with 64.04% reduction compared to placebo
Improvement in pro-inflammatory marker hsCRP, anti-inflammatory marker IL-10 & CTX II (urine)
Cartilage quality is maintained in MFT compartment (most commonly involved based on MRI T2 imaging
Less number of TKR in Stempeucel arm (1 vs. 4 in placebo) at two years follow up
In Critical Limb Ischemia (CLI) due to Buerger's Disease and Atherosclerotic PAD:
Shows significant improvement in primary end points (relief of rest pain & ulcer healing)
Improved vascularity- seen as development of new collaterals in MRA at six months follow up
